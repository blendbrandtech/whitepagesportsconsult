Who We Are

360° representation, built on trust

Headquartered in Ghana, Whitepage Sports Consult GH is a registered sports agency dedicated to representing world-class football players and boxers on the international stage. We believe every great athlete deserves a clean slate and a clear strategy — that's the Whitepage promise.

From securing lucrative global transfers and bout contracts to managing media relations and financial health, our mission is simple: empower athletes to focus entirely on their performance while we handle the rest.
