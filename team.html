Management & Team

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Philip White

Philip White founded Whitepage Sports Consult GH with a clear mission: to give footballers and boxers in Ghana and beyond access to world-class representation. With a sharp focus on transparency and athlete welfare, he leads a team that handles everything from transfer negotiations to brand strategy.

"Whitepage offers 360-degree management — from securing global transfers and bout contracts to managing media relations and financial health."
